Tips for voice journaling

  • Best spelling: use Chrome or Edge and watch text appear while you speak (live captions). Earphones are fine; the browser engine usually spells better than the backup model after you stop.
  • Set Filipino / Taglish for Tagalog journaling. If the box is empty after stop, a smaller on-device model runs — some words may be misspelled; fix them in the box before Save.
  • Speak at a steady pace, close to the mic, in a quiet room. Pauses between phrases help.
  • You can always edit the transcript, then use Save Entry for tags and emotion analysis.
